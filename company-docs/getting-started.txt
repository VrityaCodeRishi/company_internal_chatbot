AgentForce Infotech - Getting Started Guide

Welcome to AgentForce Infotech! To get your setup up and running:

1. Contact IT department (reach out to Rajesh Kumar - rajesh.kumar@agentforce.com) and fill the employee onboarding form with your employment details to get assigned a laptop. The laptop will come pre-configured with all necessary apps including FortiClient VPN, Microsoft Office Suite, and development tools.

2. Join the #agentforce-onboarding Slack channel for new employee resources and announcements.

3. Ask your reporting manager to get you assigned to your project. Your manager will add you to the relevant project channels and Jira boards.

4. Complete the mandatory security training within your first week (link shared via email from security@agentforce.com).

For any issues, contact the IT helpdesk at helpdesk@agentforce.com or call extension 1001.
